Our Story

Where Craft Meets
Affordability

FAQ

Everything You
Need to Know

Can't find your answer? Reach us on WhatsApp — we reply within 2 hours, Monday to Saturday.

What materials are Radiant Aura pieces made from?+

All Radiant Aura jewellery is crafted from 316L surgical-grade stainless steel — the same grade used in medical implants. This makes our pieces completely hypoallergenic, nickel-free, and safe for sensitive skin. Our gold and rose-gold plating is applied using a vacuum deposition process for long-lasting colour and depth.

How long does the gold or rose-gold plating last?+

With proper care, our plating lasts 1–3 years for everyday wear and much longer for occasional-wear pieces. To extend the life: avoid direct contact with perfume, sweat, and water; store in the anti-tarnish bag we include; and polish gently with the microfibre cloth provided with your order.

How quickly will my order arrive?+

All orders are dispatched within 24 hours. Standard delivery across India takes 3–5 business days. Express 1–2 day delivery is available for major cities. We also ship internationally — delivery times vary by destination and are shown at checkout.

What is your return and exchange policy?+

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return guarantee on all standard pieces. If you are not satisfied, message us on WhatsApp or email and we will arrange a free pickup and full refund or exchange, no questions asked. Custom and engraved pieces cannot be returned unless damaged on arrival.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?+

Yes! We have a dedicated wholesale programme for boutiques, bridal houses, and online resellers across India. Minimum order quantity is 50 pieces per SKU. Contact us via the form below or WhatsApp us directly for a personalised wholesale catalogue, pricing sheet, and sample request process.

Can I order a custom piece for a special occasion?+

Absolutely. We offer fully custom design services — from name engravings to bespoke bridal sets designed from scratch. Simply fill in the contact form below with your occasion details, reference images, and budget and our design team will get back to you within 24 hours.
